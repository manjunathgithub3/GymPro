# Product Requirements Document (MVP)

## Overview
This document outlines the MVP scope for the GymPro platform.

## Goals
- Provide gym membership management
- Support trainer and attendance workflows
- Enable basic reporting and dashboard visibility

## Scope
Included in MVP:
- User authentication
- Member management
- Trainer management
- Attendance tracking
- Basic payments and subscriptions

## Out of Scope
- Advanced analytics
- Full multi-branch enterprise features
- Complex AI-based recommendations
